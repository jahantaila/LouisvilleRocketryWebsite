Meet the Team

Richard Lian

President, Chief Engineer

is currently a junior at duPont Manual High School. As the chief engineer, he oversees the design, manufacturing, and testing of the rocket. He founded the rocketry team 5 years ago and has been the team captain ever since. He has 4 seasons of experience with the American Rocketry Challenge, leading the team to a successful qualification into the national competition this past season.

Himanish Lingamaneni

Vice President, Director of Test Operations

is currently a sophomore at duPont Manual High School. He has been a member of the rocketry team for the past 4 years. He is active in flight testing data analysis, and collaboration on the production of the rocket. He also is in charge of video-editing for any team video projects. In his free time, he has no free time

Vallabh Ramesh

VP of Build and Network Operations

is a student in the Jefferson County school district. Ever since he got involved in Rocketry, there was no looking back. He has always had the fascination for building, and putting things together to make it work. Together with his team-mates he has been involved in building, launching rockets, participating in Rocketry club activities, fundraising and making marketing videos. He is also involved in other activities like Quiz Bowl, Science Olympiad, Science Fair, and baseball. .

Jahan Taila

Chief Technology Officer (CTO) | Lead Web Developer

is a sophomore at DuPont Manual High School. He enjoys learning new skills and is highly passionate about technology and programming. As the CTO, Jahan oversees the testing and the successful launch of rockets. Jahan's responsibilities include rocket programming, statistical analysis of rocket data, and the development of rocket software. Jahan is also in charge of simulating rocket launches as well as developing and maintaining the Louisville Rocketry Website.
